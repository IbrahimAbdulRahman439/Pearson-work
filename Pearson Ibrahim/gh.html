About Us

Imagine Ghana, a vibrant tapestry woven with ancient traditions and breathtaking landscapes. The Ministry of Tourism, Arts and Culture (MoTAC) is the dynamic force that unveils this national treasure to the world. They're not just bureaucrats; they're passionate curators of Ghana's soul, orchestrating a symphony of cultural experiences. MoTAC's mission? To ignite economic growth by showcasing the nation's dazzling array of attractions, from sun-kissed beaches to the rhythmic pulse of traditional festivals. They're champions of sustainable tourism, ensuring that Ghana's natural wonders are cherished, not exploited. Beyond the beaches and safaris, MoTAC nurtures the creative heartbeat of Ghana, empowering artists and celebrating the nation's artistic brilliance. They safeguard the echoes of history in ancient forts and bustling markets, ensuring that Ghana's story is told with pride. MoTAC isn't just promoting tourism; they're inviting the world to experience the warmth, the rhythm, and the sheer magic of Ghana. They're building a legacy, one vibrant festival, one breathtaking vista, one captivating story at a time.

Our Mission

Promote sustainable tourism.

Preserve and celebrate Ghanaian culture.
